Slow Kooker Chicken Tetrazzini

Description

I serve this dish over homemade egg noodles. The chicken is so tender you will not need a knife...SOoooooGood!

Ingredients

  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, sliced and separated into rings
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • Â½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened

Description

Step 1

  1. Place chicken in the slow cooker crock. Top with 2 tablespoons melted butter; sprinkle with Italian dressing mix.

Step 2

  1. Cover, and cook on High for 3 hours.

Step 3

  1. Heat the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is soft, about 5 minutes. Mix in the cream of chicken soup, chicken broth, and cream cheese. Pour mixture over the cooked chicken in the slow cooker.

Step 4

  1. Cover, and continue to cook on Low until chicken in fork tender and the sauce has thickened, about 1 additional hour.
